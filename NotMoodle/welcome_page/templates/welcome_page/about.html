About NotMoodle

NotMoodle is a lightweight Learning Management System focused on flexible, self-paced learning. We prioritize shorter, targeted lessons and clear outcomes over bloat. Instructors can build courses and lessons quickly; students learn at their own pace with a distraction-free experience.

  • Faster authoring with simple, consistent workflows
  • Accessible, mobile-first UI with WCAG-conscious components
  • Transparent enrollment and lesson progression

Our Mission

To help educators deliver focused, high-retention learning experiences and empower students to progress with clarity and confidence.
